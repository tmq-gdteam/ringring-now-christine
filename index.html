Use your own voice?

RingRing.now can translate your speech so others hear it in their language, while keeping the natural feel of your voice.

  • Processing happens live, during the call.
  • It's optional — a clear, high-quality neutral voice works just as well.
  • You can switch to the neutral voice anytime, in one tap.
